About

Sadrush Anand is a passionate Physics educator with over 8 years of experience teaching secondary and senior-secondary students. He specialises in transforming abstract physical concepts into intuitive, real-world learning experiences that spark curiosity and build lasting understanding.

Teaching Philosophy

Every student can understand physics when it's connected to the world around them. My classrooms prioritise inquiry, hands-on experimentation, and conceptual clarity over rote memorisation — helping learners think and reason like scientists.

Areas of Expertise

Mechanics, electromagnetism and modern physics; competitive-exam foundation (JEE/NEET); curriculum design; and integrating educational technology into everyday teaching.

Passion & Interests

Beyond the classroom, I mentor school science-fair teams, run a student robotics club, and actively share teaching resources with the educator community to make quality STEM learning accessible to all.
